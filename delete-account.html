Delete Your LoveCipher Account

This page explains how to permanently delete your LoveCipher account and all data associated with it.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile: name, username, email, phone number, profile picture
  • Your emoji-to-letter cipher mapping
  • Your pairing relationship (your partner will be unpaired)
  • Your Firebase Authentication record

What is retained (if any)

  • Anonymized error logs may be kept for up to 30 days for service reliability.
  • No personally identifiable information is retained beyond the deletion request.

How to delete your account

Send an email from your registered LoveCipher email address to:

lovecipher.support@gmail.com

Subject: Delete my account

Why an email request? We require email verification from your registered address to confirm the request is genuine and to prevent unauthorized deletion of someone else's account.

You will receive an acknowledgement within 48 hours and your account and data will be fully deleted within 7 days.

Step-by-step

  1. Open your email app and sign in with the email address you registered with LoveCipher.
  2. Compose a new message to lovecipher.support@gmail.com.
  3. Set the subject to Delete my account.
  4. In the body, you can leave it blank or add any additional context (e.g. "Please delete my account immediately").
  5. Send.
  6. Wait for confirmation. Deletion completes within 7 days.

If you can no longer access your registered email

Email us anyway from any address, include your username, and describe the situation. We will verify identity through other means before processing the deletion.
